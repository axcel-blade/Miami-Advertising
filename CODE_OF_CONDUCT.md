# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and professional experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Expected behaviour:**
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project

**Unacceptable behaviour:**
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any conduct that would be considered unprofessional

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported by contacting the project maintainer at **info@miamiadv.ae**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.
